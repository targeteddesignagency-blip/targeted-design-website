## Session Format

**Pre-Work (Dept Lead):**
- Identify 1 improvement area
- Prepare 2-min briefing

**Discussion (15 min):**
- Problem statement (2 min)
- Proposed solution (3 min)
- Team input (5 min)
- Decision + owner (5 min)

**Implementation (1h):**
- Owner executes improvement
- Document in SOP.md or relevant file
- Post completion note in forum

**Log:** `memory/improvements/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
**Metrics:**

- Time saved (hours/week)
- Errors prevented (count)
